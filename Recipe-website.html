Loaded baked potato



Ingrediants Directions
4 large russet potatoes Preheat oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit.
1/4 cup Country Crock Original,
sour cream, freshly chopped chives		 Prick potatoes all over with a fork and wrap tightly in foil.
2 green onions, thinly sliced,
white and green parts divided		 Bake potatos till soft, around 1 hour.
3/4 cup shredded cheddar Unwrap potatoes and cut lengthwise slits on the tops of each.
6 crumbled slices of crispy bacon Scoop out flesh from center of each potato into a medium bowl.
Kosher salt Add Country Crock Original, sour cream, chives, green onion whites,
half the chedder, half the bacon, then season with salt and pepper.
Freshly ground black pepper Spoon filling back into each potato to form a mound, then sprink
remaining cheese, bacon and scallion greens, then let stand 5 minutes.

Mozzarella sticks


Ingrediants Directions
1/4 cups flour Whisk eggs and milk together
1 cup of Italian style breadcrumbs Coat each piece of cheese with flour,
then dip in egg and then roll in bread crumbs
2 eggs Dip in egg and crumbs again
1 tablespoon milk Freeze before frying
1 lb mozzarella cheese,
cut into 3/4 in. X 3/4in. strips		 Heat oil in skillet and cook sticks for about a minute on each side,
or until golden and before cheese starts to leak.
1 cup vegetable oil Drain on paper towels
1 cup jarred pizza sauce
or 1 cup marinara sauce		 Serve with sauce for dipping

Fresh Salsa


Ingrediants Directions
3 tablespoons chopped onion (finely chopped) Strain chopped onions and garlic with
2 cups of boiling water then let drain.
2 small cloves garlic (minced) Combine onions and garlic with chopped
tomatoes, peppers, cilantro, and lime juice.
3 large ripe tomatoes
(peeled, seeds removed, chopped)		 Add salt and Freshly ground black pepper.
2 chile peppers (hot or mild) Refrigerate for at least 2 hours to blend flavor.
2-3 tablespoons chopped cilantro Serve as a dip or a condiment with mexican style dishes.
11/2-2 tablespoons lime juice, salt, and pepper *Can be refrigerated for up to a week.*

Lemonade


Ingrediants Directions
6 lemons Juice lemons to make 1 cup of lemon juice.
6 cups cold water combine lemon juice, white sugar, and cold water in gallon pitcher then stir.
1 cup white sugar Chill and serve over ice

Chocolate chip cookies


Ingrediants Directions
1 cup softened butter Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
1 cup white sugar and packed brown sugar Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth
2 eggs Beat in eggs one at a time.
2 teaspoons vinilla extract stir in vanilla.
3 cups all-purpose flour Dissolve baking soda in hot water.
1 teaspoon baking soda Add baking soda water to batter with salt.
2 teaspoons hot water Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts.
1/2 teaspoon salt Place cookie dough onto ungreased pans via large spoonfuls.
2 cups semisweet chocolate chips Bake for about 10 in preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.
